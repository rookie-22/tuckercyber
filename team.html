Steve's academic achievements are complemented by his practical experience. With a Cum Laude MBA from UNC-Charlotte and a B.S. in Industrial Management from Clemson University. Steve is committed to helping brands establish a proactive security culture based on trust. With a background as a consultant and full-stack software developer for global companies, Steve has played a pivotal role in safeguarding cybersecurity programs worldwide. Leveraging his expertise in cybersecurity with Fortune 500 companies, Steve offers practical and manageable solutions to protect your assets, brand, data, and business.
